Help on product types

Product variations are defined here from within the “product types”. For each kind of product variation you can manage fields like categories, images and just about anything that is a field for Drupal.

The _product display nodes_ link these product types so you can add them inline. Generally, in Commerce Kickstart, products are created from the add product screen.
